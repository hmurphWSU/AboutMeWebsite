Work

As I am only 19, I don't really have much work experience. However, I would say I still have less work experience than the average 19 year old. I have only worked at one place, with that one place being Lagoon. I worked there for one season before I decided that I didn't want to come back because I really was not a fan of how things were run in there. In case you don't know the Lagoon application process, you apply for what area of the park that you want to work in whether it be foods, rides, ticketing, etc. Once you choose which area you want to work at, you have to choose two potential areas in the park to work. For example, I chose to work in foods, and for my two areas I chose carousel, which is the food stands surrounding the carousel toward the southern end of the park, and the Arby's toward the middle of the park. I never worked at the Arby's, only in the carousel area. However, there are things called office shift where instead of going to your normal area, they assign you anywhere that needs help, which resulted in me working in a few different areas, most memorably the Swig at Laguna Beach.
